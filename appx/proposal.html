When Economics and DS Intersect…

Author

(DIC) David, Isauro, and Katherine

Title: AI Usage For US Companies

  • Short Description of the Project: With AI usage soaring in recent years, our way of life has drastically changed. For this project, we will observe how this surge has impacted businesses across the US.

  • 2-3 General Research Questions:

    1. How has company AI usage changed over time?

    2. How does company AI usage vary by location, such as state or urban/rural status?

    3. How might company AI usage vary by internal factors, such as company size and ownership?

  • Reasons/Inspirations Behind this Project: Our group is composed of Economics majors, so we want to do a project that is related to our field. Additionally, we want to focus on recently developing news in order to make our project as relevant as possible, which is how we landed on AI usage as our topic.

  • One Potential Dataset: Annual Business Survey (ABS) Data 2017-2023

Communication Plan: We will communicate primarily through GChat, and each team member will respond as soon as possible, with more leeway given during holidays and weekends. We will set up meetings outside of class on a needs-based basis, with all members having allocated 11am-12pm on Fridays for such meetings. More meetings may be scheduled depending on the workload of a milestone.

Roles:

  • Primary Point of Contact: Katherine Manuel

  • Technical Lead: David Torres

  • Style Lead: Isauro Reyes

Conflict Resolution SOP: If a team member misses a deadline, the other team members will reach out to them and determine how to proceed (i.e. if they need extra help, if we as a team need to request an extension, etc.). The matter will only be elevated to the professor if absolutely necessary, which we do not anticipate. For evenly split decisions where the team doesn’t know how to proceed, we will flip a coin/decide randomly. However, we don’t expect this to be an issue since there are 3 of us.
